What is escrow & how does it work? Escrow is a legal agreement between two parties for a third party to hold onto money or assets until certain conditions are met. Think of escrow as a mediator that reduces risk on both sides of a transaction. In the case of home buying, it would be the sale, purchase and ownership of a home.
